病人心跳停了才器捐，蘇清泉怎能說台大強摘器官？

還原該研究的原著論文 (柯文哲等人, 2000年)。
有興趣的人可以下載全文來閱讀：
http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10770421

"After the donor's asystole was confirmed by the electrocardiogram (EKG) strip recording, the ECMO support was set up through the right femoral veno-arterial route, an occlusion balloon catheter was inserted through the left femoral artery to occlude the thoracic aorta, and bilateral femoral arteries were ligated. Usually, the ECMO could begin within 10 min after the donor's asystole."

這段話意思是，必須以心電圖確認病患進入心跳休止（asystole）狀態後才裝上葉克膜。
